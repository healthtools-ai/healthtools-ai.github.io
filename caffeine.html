About Caffeine

The FDA considers 400 mg/day safe for most healthy adults — roughly 4 cups of brewed coffee. Caffeine has a half-life of ~5 hours, so afternoon intake can affect sleep.

Pregnant women should limit to ~200 mg/day. Children and teens should consume significantly less.
