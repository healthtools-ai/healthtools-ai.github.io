How BMI is Calculated

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²). The WHO classifies BMI as:

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: ≥ 30

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. It doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, or body composition.
