How Macros Are Calculated

After calculating your TDEE, we split calories into macronutrients:

  • Protein: 30% of calories (4 cal/g) — essential for muscle repair
  • Fat: 25% of calories (9 cal/g) — supports hormones and absorption
  • Carbs: 45% of calories (4 cal/g) — primary energy source

These ratios are a balanced starting point. Athletes or keto dieters may adjust.
