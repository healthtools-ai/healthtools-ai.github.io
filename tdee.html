About TDEE

TDEE estimates how many calories you burn per day. It's calculated by multiplying your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) by an activity factor.

Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:

  • Male BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) − 5 × age − 161 + 166
  • Female BMR = 10 × weight(kg) + 6.25 × height(cm) − 5 × age − 161

To lose weight, eat ~500 calories below your TDEE. To gain, eat ~300-500 above.
